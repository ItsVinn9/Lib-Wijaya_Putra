How do I become a member of the library?

To become a member, you need to visit our library with a valid ID card and proof of address. Fill out the registration form available at the front desk or register online through our website. Membership is free for students and has a nominal fee for others.

How many books can I borrow at once?

Regular members can borrow up to 5 books at a time for a period of 14 days. Premium members can borrow up to 8 books for 21 days. Digital resources have no limit on the number you can access simultaneously.

How do I access e-books and digital resources?

You can access our digital resources through our online portal. Log in with your membership credentials, browse the digital catalog, and download or stream the resources you need. We offer e-books, audio books, research papers, and multimedia content.

What are the library operating hours?

Our physical library is open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. The digital library is accessible 24/7.

What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns incur a fine of Rp 5,000 per day per book. If a book is more than 30 days overdue, your borrowing privileges may be suspended until the book is returned and fines are paid. You can renew books online or by phone to avoid late fees.

Can I suggest books for the library to purchase?

Yes! We welcome book suggestions from our members. You can submit your recommendations through our website's suggestion form, email us directly, or fill out a suggestion card available at the library. We review all suggestions monthly and add new titles based on demand and budget availability.

Does the library offer inter-library loans?

Yes, we participate in an inter-library loan program with other libraries in the region. If we don't have a specific book you need, we can request it from another library. This process typically takes 3-7 business days, and there may be a small fee for this service.
